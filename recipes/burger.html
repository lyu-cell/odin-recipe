Description

Burgers are a quintessential part of American cuisine that have been around for decades, and with good reason. The versatility of burgers is truly remarkable, as they can be customized to suit any taste preference. Whether you like yours with classic toppings like lettuce, tomato, and onion or with more gourmet toppings like avocado or caramelized onions, there's a burger for everyone.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean and 20% fat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 leaves of lettuce
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 tablespoons of ketchup
  • 4 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 4 teaspoons of yellow mustard

Instructions to make Burger:

  1. Preheat a grill or a grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into four equal parts and shape them into patties about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Season both sides of the patties generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill the patties for 4 to 5 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through to your desired level of doneness.
  5. In the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese to each patty to melt.
  6. While the patties are cooking, prepare the buns by toasting them on the grill or in the oven.
  7. Assemble the burger by spreading ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard on the bottom bun. Top with a leaf of lettuce, a slice of tomato, a few slices of red onion, and the cooked patty with melted cheese.
  8. Finish with the top bun and serve immediately.
