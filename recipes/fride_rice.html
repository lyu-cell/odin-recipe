Fried Rice

Description

Rice is the ultimate family-friendly dish that yields maximum flavor without fuss. Loaded with tender sauteed veggies and delicious bits of scrambled egg, this top-rated recipe makes it easy to recreate a takeout favorite from home in just 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Oil or butter
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (such as onions, carrots etc)
  • Meat or seafood
  • Soy sauce or other seasonings

Instructions to make Rice

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add 1-2 tablespoons of oil and swirl to coat the pan.
  2. Add the beaten eggs to the skillet and scramble them until they are fully cooked. Remove the eggs from the pan and set them aside.
  3. Add another tablespoon of oil to the skillet and swirl to coat the pan. Add the chopped onion and cook until it starts to soften, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger (if using) to the skillet and cook for another 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  5. Add the frozen peas and carrots to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes, until they are heated through.
  6. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir to combine it with the vegetables. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is heated through.
  7. Add the cooked eggs and protein (if using) to the skillet and stir to combine with the rice and vegetables.
  8. Add 3-4 tablespoons of soy sauce (or more to taste) and black pepper to the skillet and stir everything together until it is evenly distributed.

  10. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until everything is heated through and the rice starts to develop a slightly crispy texture.
  11. Garnish with sliced scallions and serve hot.
